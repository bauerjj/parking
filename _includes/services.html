CC&R Compliance Patrol

Our licensed patrol officers conduct thorough community inspections to identify and document CC&R violations. From landscaping infractions to unauthorized modifications, we provide detailed reports with photographic evidence so your board can take action with confidence.

  • Scheduled and random patrol inspections
  • Photographic documentation of all violations
  • Detailed digital reports delivered to management
  • Consistent enforcement drives community compliance

Parking Enforcement

We enforce your community's parking rules without the hassle of towing. Our officers identify fire lane violations, expired visitor passes, vehicles without permits, and other parking infractions — issuing warnings and citations to drive compliance.

  • Fire lane and no-parking zone enforcement
  • Visitor parking time-limit monitoring
  • Permit verification patrols
  • Warning and citation issuance (no towing)
All Desert Standards patrol officers are licensed unarmed security guards through the Arizona Department of Public Safety, ensuring professionalism and legal compliance on every patrol.
