Security Patrol Officer

Phoenix, AZ Metro Area Full-Time / Part-Time
Patrol

Desert Standards patrol officers are the backbone of our operation. You'll conduct nightly patrols of HOA communities and commercial properties — enforcing parking rules, documenting CC&R violations, and providing a visible, professional presence. Every shift is GPS-tracked and recorded on Axon® body cameras.

What You'll Do
  • Conduct scheduled patrol routes through assigned communities
  • Identify and document parking violations and CC&R infractions
  • Issue warnings and citations using our mobile software platform
  • Capture photographic evidence and detailed notes for each violation
  • Wear and operate Axon body cameras throughout every shift
  • Interact professionally with residents, property managers, and board members
Requirements
  • Must be a U.S. citizen
  • Must hold or be willing to obtain an Arizona DPS unarmed guard license
  • Valid Arizona driver's license with clean driving record
  • Reliable personal vehicle
  • Comfortable working evening/night shifts (typical shifts: 6 PM – 2 AM)
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to follow procedures consistently
  • Professional demeanor — you represent the community's standards
What We Provide
  • Axon body camera equipment and all patrol supplies
  • Paid training on our software platform and patrol procedures
  • Consistent, scheduled routes — no guessing your assignments
  • Mileage reimbursement
